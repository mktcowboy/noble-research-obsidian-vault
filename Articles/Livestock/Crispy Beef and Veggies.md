# Crispy Beef and Veggies

> Browse: [[Maps/Topics/Livestock Articles|Topic: Livestock]] | [[Maps/Sections/Livestock - Grazing Planning|Section: Grazing Planning]]
> Original: [Noble.org](https://www.noble.org/legacy/2021-winter/crispy-beef-and-veggies/)
> Concepts: [[Concepts/Cattle and Livestock|Cattle and Livestock]], [[Concepts/Grazing|Grazing]]
> Keywords: [[Keywords/recipe|recipe]]

Sheri Glazier, The Dirt Road Dietitian, offers a fresh take on cubed steak with this quick and easy family-favorite recipe.

## Ingredients:

- 4 beef cubed steaks (about 1 pound)

- 2 tablespoons olive oil

- 1/4 cup corn starch

- Salt and pepper, to taste

- 1 zucchini

- 8 ounces fresh mushrooms

- 1 onion

- 1 red bell pepper

- 1 yellow bell pepper

For sauce:

- 1/4 cup soy sauce

- 1/4 cup honey

## Instructions:

1. Place 1/4 cup corn starch in bowl and season with salt and pepper.

1. Slice cube steaks into strips.

1. Coat steak slices in corn starch and leave in bowl to absorb for a few minutes.

1. Heat 1 tablespoon of the olive oil in non-stick skillet. Working in batches, brown and crisp up steak slices.

1. While the steak is cooking, prep and slice vegetables as desired for sautéing.

1. Once steak is all cooked, place cooked steak in a bowl and keep warm.

1. Add another 1 tablespoon oil to skillet and sauté vegetables.

1. While vegetables are cooking, mix honey and soy sauce together in small bowl.

1. Add the sauce to the vegetables in skillet and stir.

1. Add the cooked steak back to the skillet, stir, and let simmer in vegetables and sauce.

1. Serve immediately with your favorite sides and watch it disappear!
