# In the Kitchen: BBQ Steak and Onion Pizza

> Browse: [[Maps/Topics/People Articles|Topic: People]] | [[Maps/Sections/People - Recipes and Cooking|Section: Recipes and Cooking]]
> Original: [Noble.org](https://www.noble.org/legacy/2022-fall/in-the-kitchen-bbq-steak-and-onion-pizza/)
> Keywords: [[Keywords/recipe|recipe]]

Turn leftover steak or any other favorite protein into this quick and easy barbecue pizza from Sheri Glazier.

## Ingredients:

- Pizza dough (homemade or store-bought)

- 1 tablespoon olive oil

- 1/4 cup plus 2 tablespoons barbecue sauce

- 1 cooked ribeye, or other steak, sliced thin

- 1 red onion, sliced thin

- 1 green bell pepper, sliced

- 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese

- Balsamic glaze for drizzling

- Parchment paper to line pizza pan

Your favorite barbecue sauce is the base for a pizza that makes use of leftover steak, thinly sliced onion and peppers, plus lots of yummy mozzarella cheese.

## Instructions:

1. Preheat oven to 425° F.

1. Mix the thin steak slices together with 1/4 cup (or less) barbecue sauce.

1. Roll out pizza dough onto parchmentpaper-lined pizza pan.

1. Brush olive oil onto dough, then brush on about 2 tablespoons of barbecue sauce.

1. Add the barbecue steak to the pizza, evenly dispersed.

1. Add the mozzarella cheese, a little or A LOT, it’s up to you!

1. Top pizza with thinly sliced onion and green peppers.

1. Bake for 15-20 minutes, until crust is golden brown and cheese is melted.

1. If it suits your fancy, drizzle the baked pizza with a balsamic glaze.

1. Serve with your favorite vegetable or fruit side.

A balsamic glaze tops off this quick and tasty homemade pizza.
